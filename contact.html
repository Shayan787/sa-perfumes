Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened and unused products. Contact our support team for return authorization.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within Pakistan. Express shipping is available for next-day delivery.

Can I sample fragrances before buying?

Yes! We offer sample sets of our most popular fragrances. Visit our store or order online.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. All payments are secure.
